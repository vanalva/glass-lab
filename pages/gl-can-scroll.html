Demo · Spline + Scroll

Aluminum cans, rotating on scroll

Scroll the page to drive Spline scene transforms. Mirrors the drmush product-page pattern: a sticky 3D scene next to a tall content column, with scroll progress mapped to per-object rotation and position.

Composition

Three aluminum cans staged in 3D. As you scroll, each can rotates independently with a small phase offset between them, so they sweep around the camera in a coordinated arc.

Driver

A single ScrollTrigger watches the section. Its progress (0 to 1) drives the Y-axis rotation directly, no easing. Each can also gets a small position bob to add weight to the motion.

Why this matters

This is the exact source-side shape that Flowriter needs to map to Webflow's native PLUGIN_SPLINE action items. Each onUpdate mutation becomes a keyframe in a SCROLL_PROGRESS continuous action group, targeting each can by its scene UUID.

Open question

Spline scene objects are identified by UUIDs in PLUGIN_SPLINE actions, but source code references them by name. Either we parse the .splinecode at conversion time, or we ask the user to paste a name→UUID mapping in Flowriter. The next iteration will pick one.
