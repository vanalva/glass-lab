Materia. Luz. Precision.

Vidrio decorativo y arquitectonico de laboratorio — Panama

Absence used as structure.

We work at the intersection of material and perception. Glass is not treated as a product but as a system of depth, texture, and light. Each type is driven by objective data and evaluated through sensory criteria. It is detached from the conventional and instead processed through material intuition. What cannot be seen is studied, structured, and made material.

Depth defined by what is not present.
